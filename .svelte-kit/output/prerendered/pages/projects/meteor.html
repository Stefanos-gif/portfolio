Meteor Counter

Track your observing rate: enter your name, meteors seen, and minutes observed. See your meteors/hour, compare to an expected 60/hr baseline, and check the live leaderboard.

Open Live App GitHub Repo

How it works

  1. Enter your name, meteors counted, and minutes observed.
  2. The app computes your meteors/hour and compares to an expected 60/hr line.
  3. Leaderboard sorts by rate; green is above average, red is below.

Why it’s handy

Simple inputs, quick rates, crowd leaderboard, and a clear baseline make it great for group meteor watches and outreach nights.
